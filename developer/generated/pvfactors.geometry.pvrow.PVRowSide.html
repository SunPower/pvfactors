pvfactors.geometry.pvrow.PVRowSide

class pvfactors.geometry.pvrow.PVRowSide(list_segments=[])[source]

A PV row side represents the whole surface of one side of a PV row. At its core it will contain a fixed number of PVSegment objects that will together constitue one side of a PV row: a PV row side can also be “discretized” into multiple segments
